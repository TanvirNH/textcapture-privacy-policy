PRIVACY POLICY FOR TEXTCAPTURE

Last Updated: January 2026

1. DATA COLLECTION

TextCapture does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, user information, or analytics. The app operates entirely on your local machine.

2. LOCAL STORAGE

All captured text is stored locally on your computer. No data is sent to external servers.

3. PERMISSIONS

TextCapture requests screen capture permission only to allow you to capture text from your screen. This is processed locally.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

TextCapture does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking software.

5. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date.
